## What is Ketamine and Its Uses in India?
Ketamine is a dissociative anesthetic medication primarily used in medical settings for sedation, pain management, and treatment-resistant depression. In India, it's classified as a Schedule X drug, meaning it requires strict regulatory oversight. As of 2026, its medical applications have expanded, including off-label uses in veterinary medicine and emergency care.

India's pharmaceutical industry produces high-quality ketamine generics, making it accessible through legitimate channels. However, due to its potential for misuse, it's tightly controlled to prevent recreational or unauthorized use.

### Medical Applications of Ketamine
- **Anesthesia**: Commonly used in surgeries, especially in remote or resource-limited areas.
- **Mental Health**: Emerging research supports low-dose ketamine for depression, with clinics in major cities like Mumbai and Delhi offering supervised treatments.
- **Pain Relief**: Effective for chronic pain conditions, administered under medical supervision.

Yes, for medical use with a prescription. Recreational use is prohibited under NDPS Act.

### Can I buy ketamine online in India?
Only from verified platforms requiring prescriptions. Avoid unregulated sites to prevent scams or legal issues.

### What are the side effects of ketamine?
Common ones include nausea, dizziness, and elevated blood pressure. Long-term use may cause dependency—always use under supervision.

### Where to get help for ketamine-related issues?
Contact local health authorities or helplines like the National Drug De-addiction Helpline (14446).
